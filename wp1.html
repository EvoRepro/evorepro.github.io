WP2: Sample collection and transcriptome analysis

Transcriptomes of gametes from a number of angiosperms have been analysed during the last decade (Honys and Twell, 2004; Pina et al., 2005; Borges et al., 2008), but a comprehensive analysis, spanning lower plants to angiosperms, has never been undertaken. We are addressing this shortfall by combining unpublished data sets with new ones, building on our combined expertise of plant gamete isolations (Englhart et al., 2017; Santos et al., 2017).

We have performed RNA-Seq analysis of gametogenesis stages and isolated gametes from Arabidopsis thaliana and the model bryophytes Physcomitrella patens and Marchantia polymorpha. These RNA-Seq data sets have been complemented with publicly available transcriptome data on additional tissue types and phases of development. Moreover we have analysed the transcriptome of the basal angiosperm Amborella trichopoda during all major stages of its life cycle, including reproduction, for the first time.
